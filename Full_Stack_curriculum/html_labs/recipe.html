Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe will guide you through making the most amazing, chewy chocolate chip cookies that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Let cool on the cookie sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
